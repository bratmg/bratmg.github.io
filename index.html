About ICAAMS

International Conference on Advanced Air Mobility Systems

The ‘International Conference on Advanced Air Mobility Systems (ICAAMS)’ series is a yearly ‘Joint VFS Chapters Event’. It now encompasses mainly the new and emerging Chapters in West, Central & Southeast Asia, and the Oceania. ICAAMS Series publishes ‘Proceedings’ compiling ‘Referred Papers’ & ‘Interactive Workshop Outcomes’.

ICAAMS focuses on the new emerging Aerospace Platforms referred as ‘Advanced Air Mobility (AAM) Platforms’, covering ‘Urban Air Mobility’ and ‘Regional Air Mobility’. The Scope of the Conference is across Aerospace and Aviation on contemporary topic areas applicable to AAM as follows, with no deviations.

Aerospace

  • Aerodynamics: Transitional flight
  • Structures and Materials: Light-weight
  • Propulsion: Alternate to Fuel including Hybrid
  • Systems: Onboard and Offboard
  • Design: Multi-rotor or Compound

Aviation

  • Operations: Flight and Ground
  • Airworthiness & Regulations: Operational and Technical
  • Airspace Management: Urban and Semi-Urban
  • Air Transport: Planning and Economics
  • Product Support: MROs and Supply Chain
